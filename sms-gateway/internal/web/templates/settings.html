Maintenance
The WiFi driver on this device can crash and leave the web GUI unreachable. A daily reboot at a quiet time (e.g. 03:00) ensures automatic recovery. The gateway restarts within ~2 minutes of reboot.

Note: all settings changes take effect after restarting the gateway (see Danger Zone below).
